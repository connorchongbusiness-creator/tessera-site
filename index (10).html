Tes·se·ra · a single tile that completes the mosaic

A tessera is the single tile set by hand that completes a mosaic.

Most admissions advice pushes every student toward the same handful of "best" schools. We think that's backwards. The right college isn't the one with the lowest acceptance rate — it's the one where your student will do their best work and feel like themselves.

So we start with the student, not the rankings. We learn who they are, find the schools that genuinely fit, and help them write essays in their own voice — sharpened, never replaced. The result is an application that reads as true, and lands where it belongs.

What we do

Full-application guidance, one student at a time

Every family is different, so the work flexes. Most students start with the first few and add the rest as deadlines come into view.

01

Best-fit college lists

A list built around who your student actually is — academics, budget, distance, environment, and ambition — with reach, target, and likely schools that all make sense.

02

Application strategy

A term-by-term plan so nothing is rushed: testing windows, recommendation asks, early vs. regular decisions, and every deadline met with room to spare.

03

Personal statement coaching

The heart of the work. We help students find the real throughline of their story and write it well — their voice, their experiences, made clear and compelling.

04

Supplemental & "why us" essays

The school-specific essays that decide the close calls — researched program by program so each one reads like it could only have been written for that school.

05

Interview & test planning

Mock interviews that build real confidence, plus a sane SAT/ACT timeline built around the rest of the application instead of running the whole show.

06

Activities & résumé

Shaping the activities list and résumé so a full, busy high school record reads as clearly and impressively as it actually deserves to.

How it works

Four steps, start to submit

A clear path through a process that usually feels like fog — so the student stays calm and in control the whole way.

01

Discover

We get to know the student — transcript, interests, constraints, the schools they dream about and the ones they've never considered.

02

Shape

We build the college list and the application plan, and find the narrative that ties the whole thing together.

03

Refine

Drafts, edits, and honest feedback. Essays get sharper; the student gets more confident with every pass.

04

Place

Final review, submission, and a steady hand through decisions, waitlists, and the choice itself.

What we believe

The work is held to three rules

Fit over prestige

The best school is the one where your student thrives — not the one with the most impressive sticker. We'll say so even when it's not what you hoped to hear.

The student's own voice

Essays are coached, never ghostwritten. Admissions officers can tell, and so can we. What we build is honest, which is exactly what makes it strong.

A small roster

Real attention means a deliberately limited number of families each cycle. When you're working with us, you're not waiting in a queue.
